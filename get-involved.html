Get Involved

At ComChess for Future, we believe that every child deserves access to quality education, no matter where they come from. Our mission thrives because of the dedication and passion of people like you. There are many ways you can join our journey and make a difference:

Volunteer with Us

  • Become part of our teaching team and share your knowledge of computers, chess, or soft skills. The activity schedule will be updated once new teaching sessions are planned. If you meet the requirements, you will be contacted when the project needs your support.
Join Us

Partner with Us

  • Schools, organizations, and companies are welcome to collaborate with us to expand our reach and bring even more opportunities to children in underserved communities.

Spread the Word

  • Help us grow our community by sharing our story. With over 5,000 people already following our journey online, your voice can help inspire many more to take action.
